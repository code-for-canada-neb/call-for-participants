Call for volunteers

We're looking for federal government employees to participate in 60 min one-on-one interviews and/or observational field studies, where a researcher shadows you for 3 hours at work. These studies will guide the product direction of a modern and accessible digital environment for assessing managers.

We are from Code for Canada, a national non-profit that connects government innovators with the tech and design community.

By contacting us you agree to share your information with us.
